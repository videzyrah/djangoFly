Data Analysis and Visualization

Google Workspace Automation

I've worked with a small business owner on a project that included using AppScript to auto-fill forms and emailing .pdf invoices/estimates upon their submission. This enabled tasks that were usually done in an office setting to be done in the field on a cell phone and prevented the cost of a Quickbooks subscription. We are hoping to consult other small businesses on similar setups.

Startup Weekend Pitches and Participation

Startup weekend is a SharkTank like business idea competition.

Surviving Single / Grocery Cost Estimator

-February 2017 competion

Pitch: "Surviving Single." I was unable to gather teammates to work on the idea of a meal planning/recipe site for single people.

Participation: The team I joined instead was hoping to start a brew pub. I enjoyed brainstorming with them.

Aftermath: I think at least two more brew pubs have opened up since then. I haven't given up on "Surviving Single". My original plan to have unit prices of ingredients as parameters in the recipe database seems undoable due to price fluctuations. So I am considering taking a simpler approach (i.e. a blog, lol).

Food Preparation (Cooking / Meal Planning)

One of the main struggles in my life has been figuring out how to feed myself day to day. I hope to help others in the same boat by creating recipes and/or meal plans optimized for single people. I got a mini InstantPot for christmas and I am really enjoying figuring out new recipes that I consider better than what came in the provided recipe book. Thus; Meals for Myself: Recipes, Reviews, & Research is a backburner work in progress blog dedicated to budget conscious solutions for those of us shopping and cooking for one.
